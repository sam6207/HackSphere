About the Hackathon

Welcome to an exciting journey of ideas, creativity, and limitless possibilities! We are thrilled to host this Online Hackathon under the powerful and inspiring theme — “Open Innovation.”

Open Innovation means thinking beyond boundaries. It is about breaking traditional limits, exploring new perspectives, and transforming bold ideas into meaningful solutions. Here, there are no restrictions of domain — only creativity, problem-solving ability, and impact truly matter.

Participants are free to explore any direction — whether it is building a powerful website or application, solving a real-time problem, creating a hardware-based innovation, designing a cybersecurity solution, or developing any technology-driven idea that can make a difference in society.

This hackathon is not just a competition — it is a platform to experiment, collaborate, and push your imagination to the next level. Let this event be more than just a hackathon. Let it be a celebration of creativity, technology, and forward thinking.

Rules & Important Instructions

  • Team size: Minimum 1 and Maximum 4 members.
  • Selection is on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • All team members must join the session together.
  • Prize distribution will be conducted on Day 2.
  • Team leader must attend Day 2 or inform organizers on Day 1.
  • Certificates depend on proper attendance.
  • Each team will get 15 minutes for presentation.
  • Evaluation: Code Quality, Live Demo & Presentation.
  • Final judgement: Creativity, Presentation & Teamwork.
⚠️ Any inappropriate behavior will lead to immediate disqualification.

Eligibility Criteria

This hackathon is open to students from all over India. Students from any university or college are eligible to participate.

The hackathon is completely FREE of cost. There is no registration fee for participation.

Students pursuing courses related to Information Technology, Computer Science, or any technical background are encouraged to apply.
